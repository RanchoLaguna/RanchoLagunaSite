Rancho Laguna’s HEART is proud to have a relationship with the Middle Fork Roasters.

Both, Rancho Laguna’s HEART and Middle Fork Roasters believe in the importance of the Equine Therapy and building a bridge between animals and humans. When buying great coffee from Middle Fork Roasters in a special package, you’ll help us achieve our goal – the covered arena! Part of proceeds from selling specially designed coffee packages will benefit Rancho Laguna’s HEART! Together, we can build a bridge between animals and humans.
